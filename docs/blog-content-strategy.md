### CPA Bridge Content (New Category — Added March 2026)

**Purpose:** Capture search traffic from people whose CPAs told them they need legal help. CPA firms dominate Act 60 top-of-funnel content but systematically create legal needs they cannot fulfill — trusts, property purchases, decree succession, domicile changes. These posts position Riefkohl Law as the obvious next step.

**Target audience:** Act 60 decree holders and relocatees who have a CPA but no attorney.

**Keyword strategy:** Target phrases like "Puerto Rico trust for Act 60," "Act 60 IRS audit attorney," "moving to Puerto Rico legal checklist," "Act 60 decree succession," and "buying property Puerto Rico Act 60."

## Blog Post Template — SEO Checklist

For every new blog post, ensure:

- [ ] **Title tag:** Under 60 characters, includes primary keyword
- [ ] **Meta description:** Unique, under 160 characters, includes keyword + CTA
- [ ] **H1:** Matches or closely mirrors the title tag (set via seo-fixes.html for main pages, or Squarespace blog settings for posts)
- [ ] **H2/H3 subheadings:** At least 3-4 subheadings with keyword variations
- [ ] **First 100 words:** Include primary keyword naturally
- [ ] **Word count:** Minimum 1,200 words for substantive posts
- [ ] **Internal links:** At least 2-3 links to practice area pages or other relevant blog posts
- [ ] **CTA:** At least one "Book a Free Strategy Call" or "Contact Us" CTA
- [ ] **Author attribution:** Hans Riefkohl (already handled by Squarespace)
- [ ] **Categories/tags:** Assign to correct practice area category
- [ ] **Language tag:** Add "espanol" tag for Spanish posts
- [ ] **Legal citations:** Reference specific statutes (Law 219-2012, Act 60, Civil Code) where relevant
- [ ] **Images:** At least 1 image with keyword-rich alt text
